For the safety of all clients, employees and guests, Universal has a drive-on procedure that must be adhered to by all driving on the lot.

VISITOR'S PROCESS
All visitors to the Universal Studios lot will need a daily pass. Passes can be arranged by your Production Services Representative. When you arrive at the studio gate you will be asked to show a valid state issued ID card to the security officer. The officer will then look up your pass and direct you to your destination. Please let the officer know if you go by a name other than what is shown on your ID card to help facilitate locating your pass.

In the event that a pass has not been setup you will be directed to a courtesy phone where you may contact our Pass Office to help arrange a pass with the party you are visiting. Please have the following information available for the Pass Office; name of person you are visiting, name of production or event and a contact number. We appreciate your patience as your arrival may be delayed while we get your pass approved.

RULES FOR DRIVING ON THE LOT
Please adhere to the following rules when operating a motor vehicle on the lot:
  • Park only in assigned parking space and obey all posted signs (Stop, Yield, One-Way, No Parking, Handicap, Speed Limit and painted red curbs/fire zones, etc.)
  • Maximum speed limit is 10 MPH
  • Yield the "Right of Way" to trams and pedestrians at all times (Note: The fourth car of tram swings wide)
  • When approaching a red flashing light or a flag person who is signaling a filming is in progress, follow these directions:
    • Bring your vehicle to an immediate stop
    • Proceed only when the red light is shut off or flag person waves you on
  • Golf carts may only be operated by persons with a valid state issued driver's license.
SECURITY SERVICES
The following services are offered by the Security Department:

  • Traffic (Flag) Control

  • Talent/VIP Escorts
REPORTING AN INCIDENT
To report an incident (minor traffic accident, theft, suspicious person, etc.) while on the lot please contact our Security Operations Center at (818) 777-2445. We are staffed 24hrs a day.

To contact Fire or Police for an emergency you can call 911 directly from a land line phone. If calling from a cellular phone, call (818) 777-7911. Both of these numbers will connect you with the Los Angeles County Sheriffs Dispatch Center which serves Universal Studios. The on lot Security Department will also be notified at the same time.
